LentiLab Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-03-25

1. Data We Process

LentiLab processes user-created media and related effect settings locally on device for rendering and export.

2. Data Storage

Project assets and generated cards are stored locally on your device app container. In-app purchase entitlement state is validated through Apple StoreKit.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. User-generated exports are only shared when users explicitly invoke share/export actions.

4. Third-Party Services

Purchase and entitlement flows rely on Apple StoreKit/App Store infrastructure.
