LentiLab Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 25, 2026
App Store Review Reference Ready (Guideline 5.1.1 + 5.1.2 + 5.2.1)

1. Data Processing Overview

LentiLab is designed as a local-first creative app. Image processing, parameter tuning, preview generation, and most rendering workflows are executed on-device within the app container.

2. Data We Process

  • User imported media files (images/videos selected by the user)
  • Project configuration data (effect parameters, card settings, layout choices)
  • Generated output assets (cards, previews, exported media selected by the user)

We do not intentionally collect personally identifiable information for independent server-side profiling.

3. Storage and Retention

Project and card data are stored in your app sandbox. Data remains available until deleted by user actions, app removal, or system-level data cleanup.

If system backup/restore mechanisms are enabled on your Apple account, app data may be included according to Apple platform behavior.

4. Sharing and Export

LentiLab does not auto-publish your content. Files are shared only when you explicitly trigger export or share operations.

5. In-App Purchase and Apple Services

Purchase verification and entitlement status rely on Apple StoreKit/App Store infrastructure. Payment and billing data are handled by Apple, not by independent LentiLab-operated payment servers.

6. Intellectual Property and Rights Statement

LentiLab product identity, UI assets, legal pages, and app implementation are independently created and managed by the app publisher under the corresponding Apple Developer account.

7. Contact

For legal/privacy inquiries or App Review verification requests, use the App Store Connect review message thread as the primary channel.
